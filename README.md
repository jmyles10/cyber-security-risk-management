# cyber-security-risk-management
Project Summary: Business Cybersecurity Risk Analysis

Project Title:
Business Cybersecurity Risk Analysis for a Small E-Commerce Business

Project Summary:
This project simulated a cybersecurity risk assessment for a small online clothing retailer, TrendVault Apparel. The goal was to identify digital assets critical to business operations, evaluate potential cybersecurity threats and vulnerabilities, and recommend practical strategies to mitigate risks.
Using Microsoft Excel, an asset and risk inventory was created, listing critical systems such as the e-commerce website, customer database, payment processor, employee laptops, email accounts, and cloud storage. Threats such as phishing attacks, weak passwords, malware, unsecured Wi-Fi, data breaches, and software vulnerabilities were mapped to each asset. A risk assessment matrix was developed to prioritize threats based on likelihood and impact, producing actionable risk levels from medium to critical.
Based on the analysis, a cybersecurity policy was developed that included password protocols, multi-factor authentication, data protection measures, phishing awareness training, device security, access control, and incident response procedures. The project also included a dashboard-style PowerPoint presentation summarizing findings, risk levels, and recommended mitigation strategies.
This project demonstrated skills in business technology analysis, risk assessment, cybersecurity fundamentals, Excel modeling, policy development, and professional presentation of technical information, making it highly relevant for roles in business technology administration, operations, and cybersecurity management.
